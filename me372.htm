            The sleek lines of the ASUS Fonepad 7 will draw looks from those around you. Available in stylish Sapphire Black or Diamond White, your device will be the envy of your friends.

            Splendid

            It’s all in the Details

            Color schemes come to life with the new ASUS Splendid. You’ll be amazed at textures and colors like never before.

            Professional Photography For Everyone

            Shoot professional grade photos and videos with the ASUS Fonepad 7’s front and rear-facing cameras. With its incredible 100-shot burst mode, you’ll never miss a single moment.

            Gallery

            Create and Share the Moments of Your Life

            Edit and organize all of your photos with the fluid Studio application. Crop and label photos and instantly share them with friends and family.

            Dual SIM, Dual Stand by

            The Fonepad 7 Dual SIM 3G service satisfies every mobile phone and tablet needs. The Dual SIM function also help you to handle well of both your work and personal life, you'll never miss a call again!

            High Quality Audio with SonicMaster

            The ASUS Fonepad 7 delivers high quality audio to bring entertainment to life. Your listening experience will never be the same again when you hear your favorite tracks through precise and powerful SonicMaster speakers.

            Our engineers obsessed over bringing an innovative audio package to the ASUS Fonepad 7. Dual front-facing speakers immerse you in crystal-clear audio.

            A Better Definition of Superior Sound

            Experience home theater-quality audio on-the-go with ASUS SonicMaster.

            Audio Wizard

            Tune in to Customized Audio

            Make your movies and games come to life with customized sound by AudioWizard. Adjust and tailor audio settings to suit whatever is onscreen, the difference will amaze you.

            Control Every Inch of the Screen for Any Purpose

            Calling for Your Touch

            With its gently curved lines fitting in the palm of your hand, nothing feels quite like the ergonomically-designed ASUS Fonepad 7.

            Fonepad 7 features a micro SD for up to an additional 64GB of storage.

            Create Your Own Cloud with ASUS WebStorage

            Stay connected with all of your devices, wherever you are. With WebStorage, mobile access to new or existing files is at a touch of the screen. ASUS WebStorage keeps everything right where you need it.
